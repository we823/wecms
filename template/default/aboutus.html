Shanghai Taipen Machinery & Equipment Co., Ltd. is the only operational organization founded by Taipen Group in mainland China to expand Chinese market. The company is specialized in sales, testing and installation, after sale service and spare parts of engineering machinery dedicated hose Steel wire spiral machines, Wire bobbin winders, and Rotary wire braiders that manufactured and developed by Taipen Group in mainland China region.

Taipen Group, established in 2008, is a professional manufacturer of precision devices utilized in aviation, military, and advanced rubber industries. The group has a strong design team that has majority engineers from Europe and America, cutting-edge design concepts, professional testing equipment, and strict production process. Taipen Group is a professional supplier of rubber and plastic equipment to many German, American, and Italian companies, and has passed their assessments.

Shanghai Taipen Machinery & Equipment Co., Ltd., upholding the business philosophy of integrity and innovation, wish to cooperate with all Chinese hose enterprises, helping with your product upgrading, broadening the market, and contributing to a great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation.
